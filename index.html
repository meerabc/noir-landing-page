our story

From Seed to Cup, We Control Every Step.

We travel to the highlands and farms where our beans are grown, building lasting relationships with the growers who tend each harvest by hand. Every lot is chosen for character, not convenience.

Back in Brooklyn, we roast in small batches throughout the day so nothing sits on a shelf. Our roasters dial in each profile by taste — coaxing out the sweetness, acidity, and depth that define a great cup.

Slowness is our craft. From the patient pour to the final sip, we believe coffee deserves time, attention, and respect. That is the Nōir promise.
